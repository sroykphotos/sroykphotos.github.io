ABOUT ME

Know about S ROY K Photos.

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of an avid outdoor enthusiast and action sports enthusiast – that's me! As a passionate freelance photographer entrenched in the sports and motorsports industry, my lens captures the dynamic essence of athleticism, the rush of the great outdoors, and the pulse-pounding excitement of motorsport events.

My lens doesn't just freeze moments in time; it tells a story of grit, determination, and the unbridled joy that comes from pushing physical limits. Specializing in advertisements, catalogs, press campaigns, and product shoots, I thrive on showcasing the heart-pounding action that defines the sports and motorsports landscape. From road trips that unravel breathtaking landscapes to high-energy adventures and motorsport rally events like the Rally of Himalayas and Dirt Crush 2.0 (India's first Two-Wheeler SOLO TSD Rally), I've traversed diverse terrains to bring these experiences to life through my lens.

My portfolio extends beyond the thrill of motorsports, as I've also had the privilege of covering sports events for the All India Football Federation(AIFF) Indian Super League(ISL) & Cricket Association Of Bengal(CAB) , capturing the intensity and grace of the beautiful game. Dirt, action, and adventure aren't just genres for me; they're a way of life. I embrace the challenges, immerse myself in the energy, and go to great lengths to translate the vivid thoughts in my mind into captivating visual narratives.

Join me on a journey through my portfolio, where each image is a testament to the passion that fuels my work and the commitment to capturing the heart-pounding moments that define the world of sports and adventure. Whether it's the dust kicked up in a rally, the precision of a sportsman's movement, or the awe-inspiring landscapes of a road trip, I'm dedicated to transforming my visions into a reality that resonates with the spirit of adventure. Welcome to the world through my lens – where action, dirt, and the thrill of the outdoors come to life!

- Shubhajit Roy Karmakar
